Inspection Checklist Before Refilling

  • Cylinder Body – Checked for corrosion or cracks
  • Hose Pipe – Examined for blockages and leaks
  • Nozzle – Ensures clear discharge
  • Handle & Valve Assembly – Smooth operation check
  • Pressure Gauge – Accurate pressure readings
  • Safety Pin & Seal – Replaced every refill
  • Refill Label & Tag – Updated on every unit

Our Fire Extinguisher Refilling Process

  1. Visual Inspection: Check for damage, rust, tampering
  2. Pressure Testing: Use calibrated gauges for pressure check
  3. Agent Discharge: Old agent safely removed
  4. Cleaning: Internal flushing and drying
  5. Refill: Certified extinguishing material refilled
  6. Repressurization: Nitrogen or CO2 applied
  7. Leak Check: Full air-tight inspection
  8. Labeling: Technician initials and next due date marked
  9. Quality Approval: Supervisor sign-off and approval
