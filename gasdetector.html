Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy a portable gas detector in Saudi Arabia?

Star Links is a trusted supplier of high-quality portable gas detectors in Saudi Arabia. Our gas detection devices provide reliable safety solutions for various industries, including oil & gas, construction, and manufacturing.

What gases can your portable gas detectors detect?

Our portable gas detectors can detect a wide range of gases, including carbon monoxide (CO), hydrogen sulfide (H2S), oxygen (O2), methane (CH4), and volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Contact us for specific detection requirements.

Do you offer multi-gas detectors?

Yes, we provide multi-gas detectors capable of monitoring multiple gases simultaneously. These devices are ideal for confined spaces, industrial plants, and hazardous environments where gas leaks may occur.

Are your portable gas detectors ATEX certified?

Yes, we offer ATEX-certified portable gas detectors designed for use in explosive and hazardous environments. Our detectors comply with international safety standards for maximum protection.

How often should a portable gas detector be calibrated?

Calibration frequency depends on the model and usage conditions. We recommend calibrating your gas detector at least once every six months or as specified by the manufacturer to ensure accurate readings.

Do you provide maintenance and servicing for gas detectors?

Yes, Star Links offers calibration, maintenance, and servicing for portable gas detectors in Saudi Arabia. Our expert team ensures your devices remain in top working condition.
