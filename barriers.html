Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy barriers, bollards, tapes, cones, and bars in Saudi Arabia?

Star Links is a trusted supplier of barriers, bollards, safety tapes, cones, and bars in Saudi Arabia. We provide high-quality traffic and safety control solutions for roads, parking areas, and industrial sites.

What types of barriers do you offer?

We supply various types of barriers, including water-filled barriers, metal crowd control barriers, retractable belt barriers, and plastic road barriers suitable for multiple applications.

Do you provide high-visibility bollards for parking areas?

Yes, we offer high-visibility bollards made from durable materials like steel, plastic, and concrete. These bollards are ideal for protecting pedestrians and vehicles in parking lots and restricted areas.

What types of safety tapes do you supply?

We provide a wide range of safety tapes, including caution tapes, reflective tapes, floor marking tapes, and hazard warning tapes for industrial and traffic safety applications.

Are your traffic cones suitable for highways and construction zones?

Yes, our traffic cones are made of high-quality, UV-resistant materials and are available in various sizes with reflective strips, making them suitable for highways, road construction, and traffic management.

Do you supply expandable safety bars for temporary road closures?

Yes, we provide expandable safety bars designed for temporary road closures, crowd control, and restricted access areas. These bars are lightweight, durable, and easy to deploy.
