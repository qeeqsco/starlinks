What are wrapping rolls used for?

Wrapping rolls are used for packaging, bundling, securing pallets, protecting goods during shipping, and keeping items dust-free and tamper-resistant. They are essential in warehouses, logistics, and retail sectors.

What is the difference between stretch wrap and shrink wrap?

Stretch wrap is used for securing items by stretching around them, commonly for pallets. Shrink wrap requires heat to shrink tightly around the product, often used for retail packaging and food items.

Do you supply industrial-grade wrapping rolls?

Yes, we provide heavy-duty, industrial-grade wrapping rolls designed for high-strength applications in warehouses, factories, and shipping units. These rolls offer superior stretch and puncture resistance.
