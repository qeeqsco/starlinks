What are general handling gloves used for?

General handling gloves are designed for everyday tasks that require a balance of grip, dexterity, and protection. They are ideal for material handling, assembly work, packaging, and maintenance tasks.

What types of general handling gloves do you offer?

We provide a wide range of gloves, including **latex-coated, nitrile-coated, PU-coated, leather, and cotton gloves**, catering to different work environments and protection needs.
