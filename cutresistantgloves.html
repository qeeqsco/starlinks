Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I buy cut-resistant gloves in Dammam, Saudi Arabia?

Star Links is a leading supplier of high-quality cut-resistant gloves in Dammam, Saudi Arabia. Our gloves provide excellent protection for industrial, construction, and manufacturing workers.

What are cut-resistant gloves used for?

Cut-resistant gloves are designed to protect hands from sharp objects and materials such as glass, metal, and blades. They are ideal for industries like construction, automotive, metal fabrication, and food processing.

What cut-resistance levels do your gloves offer?

Our cut-resistant gloves come in various protection levels, including ANSI Cut Levels A1 to A9 and EN 388 cut protection ratings, ensuring the right safety for your work environment.

Do you provide bulk orders for cut-resistant gloves?

Yes, we offer bulk supply of cut-resistant gloves for businesses across Saudi Arabia, including industries like construction, oil & gas, metal fabrication, and manufacturing.

What materials are used in your cut-resistant gloves?

Our gloves are made from high-performance materials such as Kevlar, HPPE (High-Performance Polyethylene), stainless steel fibers, and nitrile-coated fabrics to ensure durability and protection.

Are your cut-resistant gloves compliant with safety standards?

Yes, our cut-resistant gloves meet international safety certifications, including **EN 388**, **ANSI/ISEA 105**, and **CE standards**, ensuring reliable protection in hazardous environments.
