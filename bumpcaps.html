What is the purpose of a bump cap?

Bump caps are designed to protect workers from minor head injuries caused by bumps, scrapes, and low-hanging obstacles in work environments like warehouses and factories.

Are bump caps required for workplace safety in Saudi Arabia?

While bump caps are not a substitute for hard hats in high-risk areas, they are essential for workplaces where head protection against minor impacts is needed.
