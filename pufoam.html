Frequently Asked Questions About PU Foam Spray

What is PU foam spray, and what are its main uses?

PU foam spray is a polyurethane-based insulating foam used for sealing gaps, insulating walls, and soundproofing. It expands upon application, providing strong thermal insulation and airtight sealing for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

What are the advantages of PU foam spray for insulation?

PU foam spray provides excellent insulation, reducing energy costs by maintaining stable indoor temperatures. It also acts as a barrier against air, moisture, and noise, ensuring a comfortable and efficient living or working environment.

Is PU foam spray suitable for sealing gaps and cracks?

Yes, PU foam spray is ideal for sealing gaps, cracks, and joints. It expands to fill voids, providing an airtight and watertight seal that prevents energy loss and water damage.

Can PU foam spray be used for soundproofing?

Absolutely! PU foam spray effectively reduces sound transmission by creating a dense barrier. It is widely used in walls, ceilings, and floors for residential, commercial, and industrial soundproofing applications.

How long does PU foam spray last after application?

PU foam spray is highly durable, with a lifespan of over 20 years when applied correctly. Its resistance to moisture, chemicals, and extreme temperatures ensures long-lasting performance.

Is PU foam spray environmentally friendly?

Modern PU foam sprays are formulated to be eco-friendly, containing minimal harmful chemicals. Their insulating properties reduce energy consumption, making them an environmentally conscious choice.
